Guinea Pig

Despite their name they are not pigs and do not come from Guinea.

  • Scientific Name: Cavia Porcellus
  • Average Length: 20 - 25centimeters
  • Life Span:4-8 years
  • Habitat: Grass or other vegetation

Stout and short legged, adult guinea pigs are about 13 centimeters tall and weigh 30 to 35 ounces (850 to 1000 grams).They are popular for their pocket size and can make very good household pets since they are relatively easy to care for and are very affectionate.
